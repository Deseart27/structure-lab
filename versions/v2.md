# V2 — B2B Data Engine

## Vision

**Find, Enrich, Push.** FullEnrich is a B2B Data Engine, not just an enrichment tool.

## Navigation

Search | Enrichment | Lists | Integrations

Sub-navigation bar (second row):
- Search: People | Companies
- Enrichment: Emails & Phones | Reverse Lookup | CRM Enrichment

## Structure

### Search
- Sub-nav: People | Companies
- Results table with checkboxes
- Actions: Save to list, Push to HubSpot, Enrich (people) / Find employees (companies)
- No Export from search (export requires going through a List)
- Push to HubSpot creates a trace in CRM integration page (Recent Pushes), not a list

### Enrichment — action launcher + process monitor
- Sub-nav: Emails & Phones | Reverse Lookup | CRM Enrichment
- Each subsection is a launcher + "Recent Enrichments" feed
- Enrichment tracks **processes**, not results — results live in Lists
- Enrichment type: multi-select checkboxes (Phone, Email, Personal Email)

#### Emails & Phones
- Three input methods: CSV upload, Manual entry, **Pick from your lists**
- "Pick from your lists" lets you enrich contacts already in a list without navigating away
- Destination list selector (Create new list... or pick existing)
- Recent Enrichments shows all enrichment types
- Completed enrichments link to "View in Lists"

#### Reverse Lookup
- Three input methods: Paste emails, Upload CSV, **Pick from your lists**
- Optional add-ons: Professional email (+1 credit), Phone number (+1 credit)
- Destination list selector
- Recent Enrichments shows only reverse lookup jobs

#### CRM Enrichment
- Connected CRM card (one CRM per workspace: HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive)
- HubSpot lists table with per-list enrich buttons
- Recent Enrichments shows only CRM enrichment jobs

### Lists — unified contact container
- All contacts from all sources live here (search, CSV, manual, reverse, CRM)
- Table view: List name, Contacts count, Enriched %, CRM sync status, Owner, Updated
- Bulk actions: Enrich, Export CSV, Push to HubSpot
- Per-row actions: Enrich, Export CSV, Push to HubSpot
- "New List" links to Enrichment section
- Quick enrich available from list detail view (enrich contacts in place)

### Integrations
- CRM connections (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
- API access
- MCP

## Key design decisions

1. **Lists are containers, not prerequisites** — you don't need a list to push to CRM from search
2. **Enrichment tracks processes, not results** — "Recent Enrichments" is a job monitor, results live in Lists
3. **One CRM per workspace** — simplifies the push flow
4. **Two enrichment paths**: full experience (Enrichment section) vs quick enrich popover (from Search/Lists)
5. **"Pick from your lists"** as third input method in Enrichment — lets users enrich existing lists without navigating away
6. **Export only from Lists** — not from Search. Export requires curated data.
7. **Push to CRM from Search** — creates a trace in CRM integration page (Recent Pushes), doesn't create a list
8. **Source badges removed from lists** — source is a contact-level detail, not list-level
9. **Three contextual panels** (popovers, not modals): Quick Enrich, Push to CRM, Export CSV

## Problems this structure may create
- "Pick from your lists" in Enrichment overlaps with quick enrich on the Lists page — two paths to the same action
- Enrichment section shows all enrichment types in Recent Enrichments (not filtered per subsection)
- Lists can become a dumping ground if every action creates a list
