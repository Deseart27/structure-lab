# V3 — Lusha's Model

## Vision

**Find, Enrich, Push.** Same vision as V2, but with a clear separation between search-built lists and transactional enrichment. Successfully enriched contacts flow into a global **All Contacts** database.

Inspired by Lusha's architecture: enrichment is transactional, but enriched contacts don't stay locked in the enrichment section — they appear in All Contacts.

## Navigation

Search | Enrichment | Contacts | Integrations

Sub-navigation bar (second row):
- Search: People | Companies
- Enrichment: Emails & Phones | Reverse Lookup | CRM Enrichment
- Contacts: **All Contacts** | **Lists**

## Structure

### Search
- Sub-nav: People | Companies
- Results table with checkboxes
- Actions: Save to list, Push to HubSpot, Enrich (people) / Find employees (companies)
- No Export from search

### Enrichment — transactional, for external data
- Sub-nav: Emails & Phones | Reverse Lookup | CRM Enrichment
- You bring external data (CSV, manual entry, CRM sync), enrich it, export/push results
- No "Pick from your lists" option — quick enrich on Lists handles that use case
- Each subsection only shows its own enrichment type in "Recent Enrichments"
- Completed enrichments have **View in Contacts** + **Export** + **Push to HubSpot**
- Successfully enriched contacts flow into **All Contacts**

#### Emails & Phones
- Two input methods: CSV upload, Manual entry
- No destination list selector
- Recent Enrichments filtered to only Emails & Phones jobs

#### Reverse Lookup
- Two input methods: Paste emails, Upload CSV
- Optional add-ons: Professional email, Phone number
- No destination list selector
- Recent Enrichments filtered to only Reverse Lookup jobs

#### CRM Enrichment
- Connected CRM card
- HubSpot lists table with per-list enrich buttons
- Recent Enrichments filtered to only CRM Enrichment jobs

### Contacts — two views

#### All Contacts (default)
- Flat view of **every contact across all sources**: search, CSV enrichment, reverse lookup, CRM
- Contact table: Name, Title, Company, Location, Phone, Email, Email Status, Source
- Source badge per contact (Search, CSV, Reverse, CRM) — shows where each contact came from
- Bulk actions: Enrich, Export CSV, Push to HubSpot
- This is the global contact database — everything flows here

#### Lists
- Curated collections, built from Search ("Save to list")
- Quick enrich available on lists (enrich contacts in place)
- List view: List name, Contacts count, Enriched %, CRM sync status, Owner, Updated
- Bulk actions: Enrich, Export CSV, Push to HubSpot
- "New List" links to Search

### Integrations
- Same as V2

## Key design decisions

1. **All Contacts is the global database**: every successfully enriched contact appears here, regardless of source (search, CSV, reverse, CRM)
2. **Enrichment is transactional but not isolated**: you process data in Enrichment, but results flow into All Contacts. You can also Export/Push directly from Enrichment.
3. **Lists are search-curated collections**: built from Search, used for organization. They're a subset view of contacts.
4. **Source tracking**: All Contacts shows a source badge (Search, CSV, Reverse, CRM) per contact — you always know where data came from
5. **No "Pick from your lists" in Enrichment**: quick enrich on Lists handles that use case
6. **Each enrichment subsection is self-contained**: shows only its own job type in Recent Enrichments
7. **"Contacts" not "Tables"**: keeps FullEnrich's own vocabulary

## How the data flows

```
Search → Save to list → Lists → also in All Contacts
CSV Enrichment → All Contacts (+ Export/Push from Enrichment)
Reverse Lookup → All Contacts (+ Export/Push from Enrichment)
CRM Enrichment → All Contacts (+ Export/Push from Enrichment)
```

## Differences from V2

| | V2 | V3 (Lusha) |
|---|---|---|
| Third nav item | Lists | Contacts (All Contacts + Lists) |
| "Pick from your lists" in Enrichment | Yes | No |
| Enrichment results | Flow into Lists | Flow into All Contacts |
| Global contact view | No (only per-list) | Yes (All Contacts) |
| Completed enrichment actions | View in Lists | View in Contacts + Export + Push |
| Source tracking | No | Yes (badge per contact) |
